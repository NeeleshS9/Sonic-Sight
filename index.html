Aim

To bridge the gap between sound and sight. Sonic Sight provides real-time visual feedback on speech patterns and volume, empowering the deaf and hard-of-hearing community to speak with confidence. By turning sound waves into interactive visual data, we help users master their vocal presence and foster deeper connections with the world around them.
